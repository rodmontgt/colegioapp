Set up Android SMS - EduAppGT PRO

  • Created : 27 September 16

To start using Android SMS follow the instructions.


* Go to https://smsgateway.me/ and Create an account.


* Enter your information

* Now log in and copy the DEVICE ID data and your email and password used in the account

* Go to your android device log in to Google Play and download the SMS Gateway App

* Once installed, open it and log in with the data of your account created on the website. You can now close the App

* Now go to System Settings, SMS and select Android and enter Your email and password and the device ID and apply the changes.

Congratulations! You have learned to change the SMS settings.
